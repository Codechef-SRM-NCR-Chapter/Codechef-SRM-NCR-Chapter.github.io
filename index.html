About Us

CodeChef was started in 2009 as an educational initiative for the programming community by Directi, an Indian software products company. Today, CodeChef is one of the world’s largest and popular global competitive programming platforms preferred by student & professional programmers. In 2020, CodeChef was added under the aegis of the Unacademy brand.

  CodeChef promotes constant learning and friendly programming competitions for its users, along with the support of its large community of problem curators.

  CodeChef was created as a platform to help programmers make it big in the world of algorithms, computer programming, and programming contests. It hosts three featured contests every month (Long Challenge, CookOff, LunchTime & Starters) and gives away prizes and goodies to the winners as encouragement. Apart from these, the platform is also open to the entire programming community, especially academic institutes, and student bodies, to host their own contests.

Our Faculty Advisor

Dr. Oshin Sharma is currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of SRM University Delhi-NCR Campus Ghaziabad, UP. Earlier she worked as an Assistant Professor in PES University, Bangalore Karnataka for 2 years and JAIN University Bangalore for one year. Prior to that she has worked as a Research Scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of JUIT, Waknaghat, Himachal Pradesh. She received her B.Tech, M.Tech & Ph.D all in Computer Science & Engineering in 2012, 2013 and 2018 respectively. She has published more than 10 papers in Scopus and SCI indexed Journals and more than 5 publications in national/international conferences such as IEEE/ Springer and Elsevier Procedia and 4 book chapters. She has also submitted 2 projects to various funding agencies which are under review.

  Her research interests include Energy efficient Cloud/Fog computing, networking, industry 4.0, Machine learning towards health care and Internet of things. She is a reviewer of various Journals and Conferences such as: Int. J. of Ad Hoc and Ubiquitous Computing, Journal of information science and engineering, Parallel and distributed computing 2022.

His keen research interests and domains include, but not limit to Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He is a tech enthusiast who is avidly inclined towards solving real-life problems by applying his knowledge of Computer Sciences. He believes it is important to be the most enthusiastic person and not the most learned person in a room which keeps one open to learning in all environments.
